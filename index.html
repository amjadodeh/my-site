My Projects

Small Circles

A fully responsive full-stack React app with an Express API. Small Circles is a social platform focused on giving you control of your data. With every post, you decide who can see your content. You can make a private post that only you can see, a post to be seen by all your friends, or select from your friend list to share to. You can even send direct messages to your friends. This application is still in progress.

Tech: React, React Router, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, RESTful API, Mocha, Chai, Supertest, and Jest
